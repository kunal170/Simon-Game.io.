Rule To Play The Game

Step 1: Press On any Key To start the Game.


Step 2: On the Screen you will receive the first signal on any of the button,
you have to press that button to progress your level


Step 3: after that you will receive signal on another button or on same button,
you just have to do that you should remember the sequence of tapping
the button and adding one in your sequence as your level increases.

For e.g.

Level 1

The first signal you receive is on red button,
then you have to click on that button to progress


Level 2

The second signal you receive is yellow,
then you should follow a sequence: {red, yellow} to move up


Level 3

The third signal you receive is on green button,
then your sequence of the tapping button becomes:
{red, yellow,green}


Level 4

Then if you get signal on yellow,
then you should tap on red,yellow,green,yellow to move ahead


In short, simply you just have to remember the tapping order of button
To increase your level
